Analytics Engineer

  • QA your work, as you work (systematize the ad-hoc checks you already do)
  • Focus your QA checks only where change is detected (or potential impact areas)
  • Share data check results for PR review (proof-of-correctness of your work)

PR Reviewer

  • View and approve data checks (visibility into developer’s work and the impact)
  • Get full coverage of data impact (preset/automated checked, and developer created checks)
  • Merge PRs with confidence (you saw/know the actual impact)
